Horse Training Podcasts – Knowledge, Horsemanship & the Art of the Partnership

Horse training is the foundation of every equestrian discipline, and the pursuit of better horsemanship is a lifelong journey shared by riders across all levels and backgrounds. Whether you are starting young horses, refining the fundamentals, or developing a high-performance athlete, horse training podcasts provide a direct connection to the knowledge, philosophy, and experience of the industry's most respected trainers and clinicians.

These podcasts explore every dimension of training, from groundwork and desensitization to collection, lateral movement, and sport-specific preparation. Listeners gain access to practical techniques, training philosophies, and real-world problem-solving from professionals who have spent careers working with horses across disciplines. Topics range from colt starting and foundational development to the nuanced adjustments that separate good horses from great ones.

Beyond technique, horse training podcasts address the relationship between horse and rider — the communication, trust, and timing that define true horsemanship. Many episodes delve into reading horse behavior, understanding body language, and developing the feel and awareness that cannot be taught in a single lesson. This deeper understanding helps riders become more effective, empathetic, and consistent partners for their horses.

Rider development is another major theme. Training is a two-way equation, and the most successful competitors invest as much in their own skills as they do in their horses. Podcasts cover position, balance, effective use of aids, and the mental discipline required to train consistently over time. Interviews with elite riders and coaches provide perspective on how the best in the business continue to grow and evolve throughout their careers.

At Podcast Corral, our curated collection of horse training podcasts represents the breadth and depth of equestrian knowledge available today. Whether you ride Western or English, compete professionally or enjoy horses recreationally, these podcasts deliver insight that will improve your horsemanship and deepen your connection to the sport.
