Western Lifestyle Podcasts

The western lifestyle category covers the full ecosystem of people who live and work inside the horse industry beyond any single competitive discipline. Listeners will find episodes on running equestrian businesses — tack shops, boarding barns, stall rental operations, and the financial structures that keep those businesses viable long-term. Financial and legal content addresses the IRS hobby loss rules that threaten horse businesses, estate planning for ranch families, and how to structure a training operation for tax efficiency. Youth development content covers 4-H and FFA horse projects, helping junior riders find appropriate horses, and what parents need to know about supporting a young competitor without early burnout. Women building careers in the industry, the economics of the rodeo road, and the entrepreneurs who built businesses because horses exist — this category is the connective tissue of the western horse world.
