Cutting/Reining Podcasts

Cutting and reining are two of the most technically demanding events in western performance, and the podcasts in this category treat them with the depth they deserve. Reining content breaks down NRHA patterns maneuver by maneuver — building a correct spin, developing a big stop without sacrificing forward movement, and the lead change execution that separates competitive runs at the Futurity and Derby level. Cutting episodes address the three-way partnership between horse, rider, and cow: reading the herd, the horse's ability to rate and mirror a bovine, and what separates a Super Stakes competitor from a non-pro pen horse. Reined cow horse content covers the full NRCHA picture — fence work, herd work, and the reining go-round that makes the snaffle bit and hackamore divisions among the most complete athletic tests in the western performance industry. Breeding, training methodology, and business content rounds out every show.
