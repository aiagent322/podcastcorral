Team Roping Podcasts

Team roping is built on synchronized timing between header and heeler, and every podcast in this category reflects that depth. Episodes cover swing mechanics, reading a steer out of the chute, horse rate and speed matching, and the mental side of competing in USTRC jackpots and PRCA-sanctioned events. The business of roping gets equal coverage: arena ownership, jackpot promotion, cattle sourcing, and what it takes to build a roping operation from the ground up. Youth development episodes address matching young ropers with appropriate horses and building correct fundamentals before bad habits form. Gear discussions go deep on rope selection by weather and cattle type, horn wrap, and rigging a heading or heeling horse for peak performance. Whether you compete on a Tuesday night jackpot or haul to major events across the region, this category has content built around how you actually rope.
