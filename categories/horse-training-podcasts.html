Horse Training Podcasts

The horse training category covers the full arc of a horse's education — from the first wet saddle pad on a green colt to finishing a bridle horse for the show pen or ranch work. Episodes address colt starting methodology, lateral work and two-tracking for performance horses, and the traditional vaquero progression from snaffle to hackamore to full bridle. Problem horse episodes tackle real-world situations: horses that blow up in the alley, won't load, or have developed a broken stop from years of incorrect riding. Ground work content covers foundational skills that transfer directly to performance under saddle, including desensitization, hindquarter yielding, and building forward movement without creating a hot horse. The psychology of how horses learn — pressure and release mechanics, threshold management, and reading emotional state mid-session — runs through every episode in this category.
