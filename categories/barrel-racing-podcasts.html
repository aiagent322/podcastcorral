Barrel Racing Podcasts

Barrel racing demands raw speed and technical precision in equal measure, and the podcasts in this category cover both with real depth. Listeners will find content on building a correct barrel horse from a raw prospect, managing a competition horse through a full WPRA season, and the pattern mechanics that separate a 16-flat from a 17-plus run — rate points, pocket depth, shoulder control through the turn, and exit angle off the third barrel. Sports medicine content is specific to the discipline, addressing the asymmetrical load barrel horses carry through repetitive pattern work and what a realistic long-term maintenance program looks like on the road. The business side covers horse sales, futurity prep, and the true cost of campaigning at the pro and open levels. If you run barrels competitively, this category was built for you.
