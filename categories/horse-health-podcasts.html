Horse Health Podcasts

Horse health content on PodcastCorral is built for working horse owners, not wellness hobbyists. Episodes cover lameness diagnosis and treatment, equine dentistry, nutrition science, reproductive health, and the practical triage knowledge that helps owners make better decisions before the vet arrives. Sports medicine content is discipline-specific — joint maintenance for sliding horses, hoof care on hard ground, and ulcer management differences between horses in heavy training and those on pasture board. The content assumes you know your horse and want real clinical depth. Guest veterinarians bring board-certified specializations in lameness, reproduction, nutrition, and internal medicine to every episode. Emergency content addresses the after-hours decisions that cannot wait until morning. This category exists because real horse health decisions are made in dark barns late at night, and the right information needs to be accessible before that moment arrives.
