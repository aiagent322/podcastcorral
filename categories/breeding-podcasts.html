Breeding Podcasts

The breeding category covers equine reproduction at every scale — from selecting a stallion for a single performance mare to managing a commercial broodmare band of 40 head. Listeners will find content on the reproductive technologies reshaping the industry: embryo transfer, ICSI, cooled transported semen, and frozen semen protocols. Mare management episodes cover synchronization cycles, foaling prep, neonatal foal care, and the critical decisions required in the first 24 hours after a foal arrives. Stallion content addresses semen collection and evaluation, marketing a first-season horse, setting competitive stud fees, and what to do when a stallion grades poorly on a breeding soundness exam. The business side covers pricing flush services, building a recipient mare herd, and how social media has permanently changed stallion marketing. These shows are built for veterinarians, breeding farm managers, and serious performance horse breeders who want more than a breed association newsletter delivers.
