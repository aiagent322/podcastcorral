What Is Podcast Corral?

Podcast Corral is the equestrian world's dedicated podcast directory. We catalog every horse podcast we can find — team roping, barrel racing, dressage, training, health, breeding, rodeo, and everything in between — so horse people can discover shows that matter to them.

We're not a podcast host, a streaming platform, or an ad network. We're a directory. Think of us as the phone book for equestrian podcasts — except free, searchable, and built by horse people for horse people.

Why We Built This

The equestrian podcast space is exploding. There are over 129 shows covering every discipline, and that number grows every month. But discovering these shows is hard — they're scattered across Spotify, Apple Podcasts, YouTube, and independent websites with no central place to browse them.

Podcast Corral solves that. One directory, every equestrian podcast, organized by category. No algorithms deciding what you see. No pay-to-play rankings. Just shows and links.

Who We Are

Podcast Corral is a platform of Bridle & Bit Magazine, Arizona's premier equestrian publication since 1978. We've been serving the horse community for decades — Podcast Corral is our way of supporting the growing audio and video content space.

No pay-to-play, ever. Listing your podcast on Podcast Corral is free. Featured placement is earned by quality and relevance, not by budget. We believe every equestrian voice deserves a spot in the corral.
