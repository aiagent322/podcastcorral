About the Show

Al Dunning is one of the most accomplished horsemen in the western performance horse world. An NCHA Million Dollar Rider, AQHA World Champion, and AzQHA Hall of Famer, Dunning has spent over five decades training horses and riders at his Almosta Ranch facility in Scottsdale, Arizona.

On The Almosta Cowboy, Dunning sits down with fellow trainers, industry professionals, veterinarians, and legends of the western horse world for in-depth conversations about training philosophy, horsemanship, and the business of horses. Episodes cover everything from colt starting and cow horse fundamentals to the economics of running a training operation.

The show is a must-listen for anyone in the reining, cow horse, or western performance horse world — and for any horse person who appreciates hearing from someone who has truly done it at the highest level.

What You'll Hear

  • NCHA Million Dollar Rider interviews
  • Western performance horse training
  • Industry business discussions
  • Reining and cow horse deep dives
