About the Show

Dale Brisby — professional rodeo cowboy, social media phenomenon, and founder of Rad Rodeo World — has built one of the largest followings in western culture through humor, authenticity, and a genuine love for the cowboy lifestyle.

Rodeo Time with Dale Brisby brings that same energy to the podcast format. Episodes feature conversations with pro rodeo athletes, bull riders, ranch cowboys, and western industry figures. The tone is loose, funny, and real — but the guests bring serious credentials and genuine insight into rodeo, ranching, and western life.

The show has become a gateway for younger audiences into the world of rodeo and western culture, making it one of the most important shows in the space for growing the sport's audience.

What You'll Hear

  • Pro rodeo athlete interviews
  • Western culture and lifestyle
  • Humor mixed with genuine industry insight
  • Gateway show for younger rodeo audiences
