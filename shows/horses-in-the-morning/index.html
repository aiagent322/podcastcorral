Horses in the Morning

Hosted by Jamie Jennings & Glenn the Geek
Listen on Spotify Listen on Apple Podcasts

Horses in the Morning is the longest-running daily equestrian podcast in the world. Launched on November 1, 2010, the show has published over 3,900 episodes and counting, broadcasting to listeners in more than 90 countries. It is the flagship program of the Horse Radio Network, the equestrian world's first and largest podcast network.

The show is co-hosted by Glenn Hebert — known universally as "Glenn the Geek" — and Jamie Jennings, a former Atlanta morning radio personality turned horsewoman. Glenn founded the Horse Radio Network in July 2008 with a single show, The Stable Scoop Radio Show, and zero listeners. The network has since grown to more than 30 shows with tens of thousands of loyal listeners worldwide. Glenn is widely regarded as one of the pioneers of equestrian podcasting, having built the infrastructure that proved horse content could thrive in the audio-on-demand format years before most of the industry caught on.

Jamie Jennings was born and raised in Atlanta, Georgia, and spent years as a military wife living across the country before settling in Norman, Oklahoma, where she operates Flyover Farm with her husband Chad. A certified Monty Roberts instructor, Jamie competes in three-day eventing and has a deep passion for transitioning Thoroughbreds off the racetrack to second careers and gentling wild mustangs. Her warmth, humor, and genuine curiosity make her the connective tissue that keeps the show's community feeling like family.

The format is a daily morning show — light, lively, and entertaining — that treats the horse world the way a good breakfast radio program treats the news. Regular segments include Daily Whinnies, Really Bad Ads, Equestrian First World Problems, and deep-dive interviews with veterinarians, trainers, breed experts, authors, and horse industry figures. The show has interviewed over 6,000 guests, given away more than $75,000 in prizes, and recorded over 10,000 ad spots for equine brands. Monday, Wednesday, and Friday episodes are hosted by Jamie and Glenn, with special guest hosts covering Tuesdays and Thursdays.

In 2022, Horses in the Morning celebrated its 3,000th episode — a milestone that industry experts believe makes it the longest-running independent daily podcast in any genre. The show continues to publish multiple times per week with no signs of slowing down. Whether you're a dressage rider, a team roper, a trail enthusiast, or just someone who loves horses, Horses in the Morning delivers a daily dose of equestrian conversation that feels like catching up with friends at the barn.
