About the Show

Clinton Anderson is one of the most recognized names in horsemanship worldwide. The Australian-born trainer built Downunder Horsemanship into one of the largest training programs in the industry, with clinics, an educational method, and a massive following across platforms.

Raw with Clinton Anderson is exactly what the name implies — unfiltered, direct, and opinionated. Anderson shares his training methods, discusses controversial topics in the horse industry, and doesn't hold back on his views about horsemanship, the state of the industry, or the people in it.

Whether you agree with his approach or not, the show consistently delivers content that sparks conversation. It's one of the most-listened equestrian podcasts on Spotify and Apple Podcasts, and a regular advertiser in Bridle & Bit Magazine.
