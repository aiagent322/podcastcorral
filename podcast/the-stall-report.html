The Stall Report

Category: Western Lifestyle

A podcast covering the business and logistics of equestrian facility management — from private training barns to public boarding operations and competition venues. The host has managed large boarding and training facilities in Arizona and Colorado and brings a facility operations perspective to topics like stall rental pricing strategy, boarding contract essentials, manure management compliance, and the insurance considerations specific to equestrian properties. Episodes have addressed hiring and managing barn staff, building a waiting list culture for a desirable facility, and the capital improvement decisions that affect both horse welfare and property value. The show covers arena footing maintenance and renovation costs, the business case for covered arenas in hot-weather markets, and how to structure lease agreements for facilities that host outside trainers. Facility owners dealing with noise ordinances, zoning challenges, and neighbor disputes have found the legal and communication-focused episodes especially useful.
