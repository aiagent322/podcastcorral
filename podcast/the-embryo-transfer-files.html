The Embryo Transfer Files

Category: Breeding

A deep-dive podcast dedicated exclusively to the science and business of equine embryo transfer. Hosted by a reproductive veterinarian with 20 years in warmblood and quarter horse programs, each episode breaks down the technical side of donor mare management, synchronization protocols, recipient mare selection, and fresh versus cooled versus frozen embryo success rates. Regular guests include AI technicians, equine reproduction specialists, and breeding farm managers from programs across Texas, Oklahoma, and California. Episodes have covered topics like split embryos, ICSI (intracytoplasmic sperm injection) for low-quality stallion semen, and managing a flush schedule around a show mare's competition calendar. The podcast also addresses the business side — pricing flush services, building a recipient mare herd, and marketing a breeding program to mare owners nationally. Essential listening for breeding farm operators, veterinary students, and serious Quarter Horse and Paint breeders looking to expand their program's reproductive capabilities.
