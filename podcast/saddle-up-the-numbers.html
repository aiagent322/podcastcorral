Saddle Up the Numbers

Category: Western Lifestyle

A financial and business podcast specifically for horse owners and equestrian industry entrepreneurs. The host is a CPA who also owns barrel horses and has spent years helping clients in the equine industry structure their businesses, manage tax exposure, and plan for the unique financial challenges of the horse world. Episodes address Schedule F versus Schedule C for horse-related income, the IRS hobby loss rules and how to legitimately document a horse business, depreciation on broodmares and stallions, and the estate planning complications that arise when a farm or breeding operation is part of a family estate. The show also covers insurance — mortality, major medical, loss of use, and liability coverage gaps that leave most small horse operations exposed. Guest appearances from equine attorneys, financial planners specializing in agricultural clients, and successful horse business operators who share their financial structures make this one of the most practically useful podcasts in the equestrian space.
