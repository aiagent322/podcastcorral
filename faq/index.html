Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Podcast Corral — finding shows, getting listed, and how the directory works.

About the Directory

What is Podcast Corral?
Podcast Corral is the equestrian world's dedicated podcast directory. We catalog horse podcasts across every discipline — team roping, barrel racing, dressage, training, health, breeding, rodeo, and more — so horse people can discover shows in one place. We're not a podcast host or streaming platform. We're a curated directory with links to Spotify, Apple Podcasts, and YouTube. Podcast Corral is a platform of Bridle & Bit Magazine.
How many podcasts are listed?
We currently list 129 equestrian podcasts across 12 categories. The directory grows regularly as we discover new shows and receive submissions from podcast hosts. Our goal is to be the most comprehensive equestrian podcast directory available.
What categories do you cover?
We organize shows across 12 categories:
  • General Equestrian (38 shows)
  • Training & Horsemanship (21 shows)
  • Lifestyle (14 shows)
  • Team Roping (12 shows)
  • Industry & Business (10 shows)
  • Health & Veterinary (8 shows)
  • Barrel Racing (7 shows)
  • Western Lifestyle (7 shows)
  • Dressage (4 shows)
  • Rodeo (4 shows)
  • Eventing (3 shows)
  • Breeding (1 show)
Is Podcast Corral free to use?
Yes. Browsing the directory is completely free. There are no subscriptions, accounts, or paywalls. Just open the site, find a show you like, and click through to listen on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, or YouTube.
Who runs Podcast Corral?
Podcast Corral is a platform of Bridle & Bit Magazine, Arizona's premier equestrian publication since 1978. We've been serving the horse community for decades — Podcast Corral is our way of supporting the growing equestrian audio and video content space.

Finding & Listening to Podcasts

How do I listen to a podcast listed here?
Each podcast card has listen buttons — typically Spotify (red) and Apple Podcasts (green). Click either button and it will open that show directly in the Spotify or Apple Podcasts app on your phone, or in your browser on desktop. We don't host or stream the podcasts ourselves — we link you directly to where each show lives.
What's the difference between Video and Audio podcasts?
Video podcasts are shows that publish with a visual component — typically on YouTube or Spotify Video. Audio podcasts are traditional listen-only shows available on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, and other podcast players. Use the Video/Audio tabs on our homepage to filter by format. Many shows publish in both formats.
Can I search for a specific podcast?
Yes. Use the search bar on the homepage to search by podcast name, host name, or topic. You can also filter by category using the chips below the search bar, or browse individual category pages for a focused view.
A podcast link is broken or goes to the wrong page. What should I do?
Podcasts sometimes change their URLs, move platforms, or discontinue their shows. If you find a broken link, please email us at rexwager@aol.com with the podcast name and we'll update or remove it within 48 hours.

Submitting Your Podcast

How do I get my podcast listed?
Fill out our submission form. We need your podcast name, host name, category, at least one platform link (Spotify, Apple, or YouTube), a short description, and your email for confirmation. The whole process takes about 60 seconds. Read our full How to Submit guide for details.
Does it cost anything to be listed?
No. Listing your podcast is free, permanently. No pay-to-play, no annual fees, no hidden charges. Every equestrian podcast gets listed regardless of audience size, production quality, or budget.
How long does it take to get listed after I submit?
We review all submissions within 48 hours. We verify your podcast links are active and the show is equestrian-related, pull your artwork from Spotify or Apple Podcasts, and add your show to the directory. You'll receive an email confirmation when your listing is live.
My podcast is on YouTube only. Can I still submit?
Yes. We list video podcasts too. Include your YouTube channel URL in the submission form and we'll add it with a "Watch" button instead of Spotify/Apple links.
Can I update my listing after it's live?
Yes. Email us at rexwager@aol.com with any changes — updated description, new platform links, corrected category, or new artwork. We'll update your listing within 48 hours.
I have multiple shows. Can I list all of them?
Absolutely. Submit each show separately through the submission form so each one gets its own card and category placement.

Featured Shows

What does it mean to be a Featured show?
Featured shows get a ⭐ badge and appear in the prominent Featured section at the top of the directory. They also have dedicated show pages with extended biographies of the hosts. Featured placement is editorial — it's based on quality, consistency, and relevance to the equestrian community. It is never paid.
How do I get my podcast featured?
We select featured shows based on consistent publishing schedule, quality of content and production, relevance to the equestrian community, and existing relationship with Bridle & Bit Magazine. If you'd like to be considered, mention it in the notes field when you submit your podcast. Featured placement is never paid — it's earned by the quality of your show.

Technical & Contact

Do I need an account to use Podcast Corral?
No. There are no accounts, logins, or registrations. The directory is open to everyone. Just browse, find a show, and click to listen.
Does Podcast Corral collect my data?
We don't use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics that identify individual users. When you click a Spotify or Apple Podcasts link, you leave our site and those platforms have their own privacy policies. The only personal information we collect is what podcast hosts voluntarily provide through the submission form.
How do I contact Podcast Corral?
Email us at rexwager@aol.com for any questions, corrections, broken links, partnership inquiries, or feedback. We respond within 48 hours.
Can I advertise on Podcast Corral?
Yes. We offer banner advertising and sponsorship opportunities for equestrian brands, feed companies, tack shops, and other businesses that serve the horse community. Contact us at rexwager@aol.com for rates and availability.
I want to remove my podcast from the directory. How?
Email us at rexwager@aol.com from the email address associated with your podcast, and we'll remove your listing within 48 hours. No questions asked.
